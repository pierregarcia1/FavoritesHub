📱 Get the Mobile App
⚡
Install with one tap
Your browser supports direct installation.
Android (Chrome)
1
Open favoriteshub.onrender.com in Chrome on your phone
2
Tap the ⋮ menu (top right) → "Add to Home screen"
3
When shopping, tap Share → FavoritesHub to save any item
iPhone / iPad (Safari)
1
Open favoriteshub.onrender.com in Safari (must be Safari, not Chrome)
2
Tap the Share button ↑ at the bottom → "Add to Home Screen"
3
When shopping, tap Share ↑ → FavoritesHub to save any item instantly
Setup on your phone
Open this link on your phone:
https://favoriteshub.onrender.com

Then follow the steps for your device:
Android (Chrome): ⋮ Menu → Add to Home Screen
iPhone (Safari): Share ↑ → Add to Home Screen

💡 Saving items on mobile
Once installed, open any shopping page in your browser, tap Share, and select FavoritesHub. A save screen will appear instantly.
